About Us

Krishi Shikshya is a platform for the people who are involved in agriculture activities to gain knowledge about crops,cultivation,irrigation,farming products,tools and maintainance of products. It is the learning environment for the Nepalese people interested in agricultural activities.

There are many farmers who are not getting proper knowledge,infrastructure, tools for cultivation.In order to remove these hinderness and primitive agriculture techniques , Krishi Shikshya ra Samadhan has brought a modern agriculture platform for the people of Nepal .
